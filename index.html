Who I am

I operate below the surface. While most people interact with abstractions, I work where the kernel meets the hardware — reading registry hives, tracing syscalls, patching loaders.

On Windows, I navigate useful registry keys, weird system32 quirks, and know how to bend the shell to my will. On Linux, I live in systemd units, GRUB configs, shell RC files & scripts, and know how to use the terminal on an exceptional level.

Scripting is a native language: PowerShell, Zsh/Bash, and cmd.exe — for real automation, not tutorials.
